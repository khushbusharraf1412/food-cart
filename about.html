Welcome to Our Food Cart!

We started Food Cart with a simple dream — to bring mouth-watering street food flavors to everyone’s plate at affordable prices. From crispy Dosas to spicy Paneer Chowmein and our famous Special Thali, we serve food made with love and hygiene.

Our team is passionate about Indian street-style delicacies, blending traditional recipes with modern taste. Every item is freshly prepared, ensuring you get that perfect mix of taste, freshness, and happiness in every bite!

We believe that good food brings good mood — and that’s exactly what we aim to deliver every day!

Our Mission

To serve delicious, hygienic, and affordable meals that celebrate India’s diverse street food culture while maintaining quality and taste for every customer.
